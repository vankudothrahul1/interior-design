Interior Painting is the process of applying paint to walls, ceilings, and other surfaces inside a building. It enhances the look of a space, adds personality with color, and protects surfaces from wear and tear.

Tools in Interior Design include software like AutoCAD and SketchUp for creating layouts, mood boards for visualizing styles, measuring tools for accuracy, and color swatches to select palettes.

House Interiors like false ceilings enhance aesthetics and functionality. False ceilings, made of materials like gypsum or POP, hide wiring, improve lighting, and provide insulation.

Couches are key elements in interior design, offering comfort and style. They come in various designs, materials, and sizes to match themes like modern, classic, or minimalistic. Couches enhance the room's ambiance and provide functional seating.

Beds in Interior Design combine comfort and style. They come in various types like platform beds, canopy beds, or storage beds, each suited to different room aesthetics.

Bathtubs in interior design add luxury and functionality to bathrooms. They come in various styles like freestanding, built-in, or clawfoot, and materials such as acrylic, cast iron, or stone.
